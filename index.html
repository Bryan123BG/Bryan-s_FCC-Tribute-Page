Brief information on Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was born on 24 February 1955 in San Fransisco. In his life, he as done many noteable things, some of them are listed here:

  • Co-founder, Chairman, and CEO of Apple. Some innovative products that the company released are:
    • iPhone
    • iPad
    • iPod
  • Founder, Chairman, and CEO of NeXT. Some innovative products or software that the company released are:
    • NeXT Computer
    • NeXTcube
    • NeXTSTEP
  • Primary investor, and Chairman of Pixar. Some innovative products or software that the company released are:
    • Toy Story
    • A Bug's Life
    • Monsters, Inc.
